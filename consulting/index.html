Instrument 01 · The organizational diagnostic

BAIC™ Business Architecture & Intelligence Cycle

A thirty-day diagnostic of the business as an interdependent system. Eight domains are scored against a defined rubric using document review, stakeholder interviews, and the operational data the business already has. Missing evidence defaults to the lowest score, operational data outweighs self-report, and ties break downward.

Powered by VMOCA

Vulnerability Mechanism Outcome & Criticality Analysis, adapted from systems-engineering risk frameworks, scores 150+ failure mechanisms across 50+ failure causes as expected loss in dollars, then ranks them into a prioritized fix sequence. That is the credibility marker you walk in with: measurable architecture, not a maturity-model checklist.

What the client walks away with

A · Architecture maturity report

A ranked diagnosis across all eight domains, plus the upgrade sequence that produces the most stability per dollar.

B · Vulnerability register

A ranked list of failure modes, typically 40+, each traceable to the evidence that surfaced it.

C · 90-day operating blueprint

Sequenced, owner-tagged, dependency-aware. The installation order for the architecture the diagnosis prescribed.

D · Hosted dashboard

A live view of maturity and vulnerability posture, with quarterly re-scoring to confirm the work is sticking.

Instrument 02 · The leadership diagnostic

LDNA™ Leadership Drivers & Natural Aptitudes

A two-part instrument that scores every leader twice: their natural style, and the behavior their current role demands. The gap between the two, the Adaptation Gap, is the energy a leader burns to fit the role and the coaching that would relieve it. BAIC reads the organization as a system. LDNA reads the leaders inside it, and every profile stacks into a team interaction grid.
