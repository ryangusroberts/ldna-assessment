The eight questions.

Answer these and we'll respond within one business day with whether there's structural leverage worth pursuing.

1
About you

Required so we can respond.

2
What's your current monthly revenue?

Approximate is fine. This is for structural context, not judgment.

3
What's the next revenue milestone you're aiming for?

The next ceiling you're trying to push through.

4
What feels chaotic, slow, or unclear inside the company?

Describe what's not working, even if you can't name it precisely.

5
What systems or structures break most often?

The recurring failure points, the things that keep recurring even after you've tried to fix them.

6
How is this affecting your time, execution, and leadership bandwidth?

The personal cost. What it's costing you, your team, and your calendar.

7
If we fixed the right thing, what would change?

What does success look like 90 days from now?

8
Anything else we should know?

Optional. Context, timing, constraints, or specific questions.
