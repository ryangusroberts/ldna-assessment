The signature read

The Adaptation Gap.

Where your natural self and your role behavior diverge, energy is spent. The LDNA quantifies that load per dimension and names its shape, aligned, demand-weighted, or bidirectional. It is the daily cost of being who your role asks you to be, made visible.
